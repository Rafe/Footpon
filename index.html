What is Footpon?

 

Footpon is an Android application that rewards the window shopper with coupons to use.

  1. Walk around and your device will count the amount of steps you have taken.
  2. Choose a coupon from the surrounding area.
  3. Use your stored steps to redeem this coupon.
  4. Display the coupon to the store to use it.
